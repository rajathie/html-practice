Hello, welcome to the Adoption Center in Santa Clara. The animals on this webiste are currently available and are looking for a new home. They are all very kind and don't have any diseases. We only offer dogs, because we think that dogs are the best pets a human can own. They can be your best friend, but also want you to be their best friend. Please think about the responsibilities of having a dog. Before contacting us, think about the time and effort you have to put in, and be aware that a dog is not an easy pet to keep! The dogs can not wait to meet you and are very excited to find a new home.
